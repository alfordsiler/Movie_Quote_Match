Game Instructions

  • After pressing the start button, the timer will begin counting down and a movie quote will appear. You will have to click on the picture below of the character who says the line.
  • You earn a point if you click the correct character.
  • If you click the NEXT button, you will NOT get a point and cannot go back.
  • Once you choose a character, you will not be able to select that one again.
  • PLayer 1 will play the full round, then Player 2. Whoever has the highest score wins. Duh.
  • If you need a hint, hover over the picture and you will see a bit of the clip.
